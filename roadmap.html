Our Roadmap to Success

The roadmap below outlines the key milestones we plan to achieve as we develop and grow Quant (QNTX). We are committed to providing transparency and keeping our community updated every step of the way.

Phase 1: Initial Development & Token Launch (0–3 months)

Q1 - Conceptualization & Whitepaper

  • Finalizing the core idea behind Quant (QNTX)
  • Completing the Whitepaper with all technical details
  • Ensuring legal compliance

Q2 - Smart Contract Development

  • Developing and testing the smart contract
  • Getting the contract audited

Q3 - ICO & Token Launch

  • Launching the Initial Coin Offering (ICO)
  • Deploying QNTX on the blockchain
  • Listing on decentralized exchanges

Phase 2: Community Building & Marketing (3–6 months)

Q1 - Community Engagement

  • Building strong social media communities
  • Engaging with early adopters

Q2 - Marketing & Promotions

  • Collaborating with crypto influencers
  • Organizing airdrops and giveaways

Q3 - Exchange Listings

  • Apply for listings on major platforms
  • Listing on top decentralized exchanges

Phase 3: Platform Development & Ecosystem Expansion (6–12 months)

Q1 - DApp Development

  • Developing the Quant DApp
  • Implementing staking features

Q2 - Governance Features

  • Introducing governance mechanisms
  • Creating decentralized governance model

Q3 - Expanding Token Utility

  • Integrating QNTX for payments
  • NFT marketplace integration

Phase 4: Exchange Listings & Global Adoption (12–24 months)

Q1 - Major Exchange Listings

  • Securing listings on major exchanges
  • Improving trading volume and liquidity

Q2 - Strategic Partnerships

  • Forming strategic partnerships
  • Expanding real-world utility

Q3 - Continuous Development

  • Introducing new features
  • Focus on scalability and security

Phase 5: Long-Term Sustainability & Innovation (2+ years)

Ongoing - Ecosystem Growth

  • Expanding the Quant ecosystem
  • Integrating with traditional finance

Future - Bridging DeFi

  • Introducing stablecoins and loans
  • Adoption by financial institutions

Ongoing - Community Engagement

  • Growing and engaging with community
  • Active participation in governance

Stay Connected with Us

This roadmap will be updated regularly to reflect progress and new milestones. We're committed to delivering on our promises and are excited to have you on this journey with us.
