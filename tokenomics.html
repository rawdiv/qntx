Tokenomics: The Heart of Quant (QNTX)

At the core of any cryptocurrency project lies its tokenomics—how tokens are distributed, utilized, and managed. For Quant (QNTX), we've designed our tokenomics to ensure long-term sustainability, growth, and community engagement.

1. Overview of Quant (QNTX)

Quant (QNTX) is a deflationary cryptocurrency designed to power decentralized finance (DeFi) solutions, governance systems, and reward mechanisms.

  • Token Name: Quant
  • Ticker Symbol: QNTX
  • Blockchain: Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
  • Total Supply: 50,00,000 (5 Million QNTX)

3. Token Utility

a. Staking Rewards

Stake QNTX to earn passive rewards. By participating in staking pools, holders can receive a portion of transaction fees and other rewards generated by the Quant ecosystem.

b. Governance

QNTX token holders will have the ability to participate in decision-making for the project. This includes voting on major protocol upgrades, future developments, and changes to tokenomics.

c. Transaction Fees

QNTX tokens will be used to pay transaction fees on the network. This includes fees for sending tokens, interacting with smart contracts, and using decentralized applications (dApps).

d. Liquidity Provision

QNTX holders can provide liquidity to decentralized exchanges (DEXes) like PancakeSwap and earn fees.

4. Deflationary Mechanism

Quant (QNTX) is a deflationary token, meaning that the total supply will decrease over time through:

  • Burning: A portion of tokens burned with every transaction
  • Scheduled Burn Events: Periodic burn events to maintain limited supply

5. Token Release Schedule

  • Team & Advisors: Locked for 1 year, then vested over 3 years
  • Public Sale: Available immediately post-sale
  • Staking Rewards: Distribution starts in Q2 2025

6. Long-Term Vision

  • Cross-chain integrations
  • Decentralized applications (dApps)
  • Partnerships with leading platforms

7. Transparency and Reporting

We are committed to transparency with our community. Regular updates on token supply, burn events, staking rewards, and team vesting will be shared on our platform.

Conclusion

Quant (QNTX) is designed with sustainability, growth, and community involvement at its core. Through a balanced tokenomics model, we aim to ensure the token's value increases over time while rewarding users for their participation in the ecosystem.
